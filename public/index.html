FAQ

Common questions

How does it know my availability?
Jalendr syncs directly with your Google Calendar. It checks your existing appointments in real-time and only offers times when you're actually free.
What if the caller has a complicated request?
Jalendr is trained to handle common scenarios for home service businesses. For unusual requests, it captures all the details and notifies you immediately so you can follow up personally.
Can I customize what it says?
Absolutely. You set your business name, greeting, the types of jobs you take, your service area, and your hours. Jalendr uses all of this to have natural conversations.
Does it work with my existing phone number?
Yes. You keep your current business number and simply forward calls to Jalendr when you're unavailable—or all the time. Your customers won't notice a difference.
How much does it cost?
Way less than a receptionist or missed jobs. Get in touch for pricing tailored to your call volume and needs.
Is there a contract?
No long-term contracts. Month-to-month, cancel anytime. We keep you because the product works, not because you're locked in.
